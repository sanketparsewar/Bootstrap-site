About Us

We are an information based place for venues offering a wide array of choices based on a specific need, be it conferences, meetings, brand launches, social get together and events. Conference Venue is well equipped to serve corporates, individuals as well as entities engaged in organising events. We facilitate a process of connecting relevant partners with a prospect searching for a right venue. Our mission is to serve as an enabler to a customer for finding the right venue with a selection of attributes at the click of mouse.

Our website takes the client through a set of well-orchestrated attributes to narrow their search to a set of select venues to choose from. These attributes may vary from the category of venue, to a number of attendees, type of setting to a specific requirement filtered down to proximity of venue to various other amenities.

Each partner managing the venue listed on our website has been extensively audited to the best available information and ability for authenticity of information, we have earned credibility for maintaining high service standards and believe in providing a memorable experience to cherish.

Our team of experienced travel specialists are constantly shortlisting, auditing and updating the information with new property’s across existing as well as new locations. As part of our solution offering, we customize requests as per our client’s requirements.

We have established ourselves to become an unique platform that helps in taking informed decision, with your priorities in mind, right from filtering to finalising a venue.
